Do NOT try to impress people with technical complexity.

Instead:

* Simplify everything
* Use business pain points
* Use local examples
* Use Hindi-English mixed emotional style visually
* Focus on outcomes

Example:

❌ “We provide scalable AWS infrastructure.”

✅ “Your website won’t crash during festival sales or high traffic.”

That single change makes your marketing 10x stronger for local businesses.
