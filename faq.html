Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services, process, and technologies.

General Questions

How do I start a project with VitableTech?
Starting a project with us is simple. You can contact us via our website form, email, or phone. We'll schedule a discovery call to understand your requirements, goals, and budget. From there, we'll provide a proposal and roadmap for your project.
What industries do you specialize in?
We have experience working across various industries, including E-commerce, Logistics, Healthcare, Education, and FinTech. Our adaptable team can quickly understand specific industry nuances to deliver effective solutions.

Services & Support

Do you provide maintenance after launch?
Yes, we offer ongoing maintenance and support packages. We ensure your application remains secure, up-to-date, and performs optimally. We handle bug fixes, security patches, and feature enhancements as agreed upon.
Can you work with existing codebases?
Absolutely. We can audit your existing codebase, suggest improvements, and take over development. We often help clients refactor legacy code or migrate to modern frameworks.

Technical & Pricing

What technologies do you use?
We use a modern tech stack including React, Node.js, Python, Flutter, React Native, and AWS. We choose the best technology based on the specific requirements of each project to ensure scalability and performance.
How is project pricing determined?
Pricing depends on the project scope, complexity, and timeline. We offer fixed-price contracts for well-defined projects and hourly/monthly models for ongoing development. We're transparent about costs and work within your budget.
